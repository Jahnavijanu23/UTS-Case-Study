# UTS Case Study Project

## Introduction
This project presents a case study focusing on problem identification, analysis, and solutions. It demonstrates how structured thinking can help solve real-world issues.

---

## Problem Identification
- Lack of proper management  
- Poor communication between team members  
- Inefficient use of resources  

---

## Causes of the Problem
- Weak planning strategies  
- Lack of leadership  
- No proper coordination  
- Limited monitoring of progress  

---

## Analysis
The case study shows that without proper planning and communication, organizational performance decreases. A structured approach is necessary to improve efficiency and productivity.

---

## Solutions / Recommendations
- Improve communication among team members  
- Apply better management strategies  
- Allocate resources efficiently  
- Monitor progress regularly  
- Encourage teamwork and leadership  

---

## Conclusion
This case study highlights the importance of planning, communication, and management in solving real-world problems. Proper implementation of strategies leads to better outcomes.
